The Afritech Intelligence Hub is an enterprise-grade operational reporting and data pipeline ecosystem designed to unify manufacturing performance, sales metrics, and compliance tracking across multiple industrial facilities. By migrating from fragmented, manually handled spreadsheets to an automated, centralized architecture, the platform provides real-time data visibility, mitigates operational waste, and drives margin expansion.

Current Execution — Phase 1: Sachet Water
Our current execution deployment focuses entirely on Phase 1: Sachet Water operations. This analytical module processes daily production counts, raw material utilisation, machine downtime events, fuel/utility logs, and direct field distribution metrics. It provides leadership with granular visibility into exact yield percentages, batch variances, and shift-by-shift profitability metrics.
Revenue & Targets (DD1) — Daily and weekly revenue trends, bag-to-target tracking, cash vs bank split, and sales flow funnel.
Customers & People (DD2) — Customer revenue rankings, sales-person performance, price intelligence, and outstanding debt monitoring.
Shift & Output (DD1) — Shift-level production counts, morning vs night split, supervisor rankings, and power-source dependency tracking.
Yield & Waste (DD2) — Roll-level yield percentages, OEE metrics, film utilisation efficiency, waste trends, and machine grid performance.
Future Roadmap Deliverables
As part of the continuous rollout strategy, future modules will scale horizontally to bring the remaining factory lines online. Upcoming deployments include Phase 2: Bakery Production lines, Phase 3: Bottled Water assets, and Phase 4: Rice Processing facilities. These will share the same centralised data infrastructure to deliver a single source of truth across all business verticals.
Shared Infrastructure — All phases feed a unified Google Apps Script data pipeline with a single API endpoint delivering normalised JSON to this dashboard.
Automated Alerting — Anomaly detection (>40% KPI drop vs 7-day average) triggers instant email alerts across all factory lines as they onboard.
Cross-Factory Comparison — The Overview page will evolve into a live multi-line performance matrix once Phases 2–4 are onboarded.
Data Governance — 30-day rolling RAW_LOG retention, 90-day archive sweep, and schema registry ensure compliance and auditability across all lines.
